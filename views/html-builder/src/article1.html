How To Destroy Shields

Use Element Reactions

Superconduct
Cryo + Electro
Produces Superconduct that deals with AoE Cryo damage that will decrease the Physical RES for a time.
Overload
Pyro + Electro
Explode, dealing Pyro damage in an area. Can push back the boss and stop some of its Electro Attack.
Freeze
Cryo + Hydro
Feezes the enemy and make them idle for the time being.
Recommended Characters
Ganyu Diona Chongyun
Bennett - -

Characters with shield are also recommended as they will protect you for the constant charge damage released by the Abyss Lector.

Use A Crowd Control Character

A character with a crowd control skill is also recommended. This skill will give you time to easily gather both and freeze them while giving constant damage.
